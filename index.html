    About Us

    Who Are We?

    Arabian Group is a diversified investment holding company that specializes in small and medium enterprises (SME) investments and large enterprise investments. Our group is formed of businessmen all of whom are well-established with a set of investments across distinct sectors such as construction, real estate, Oil& Gas, petrochemicals, mining, agribusiness, education, export& import, industrialization of agriculture& meat production and processing, healthcare, manufacturing, financial services, pharmaceutical industry, technology, and media& telecommunications. Our Group relies on collective efforts utilizing the power of the unified of different business entities under one management. Our partners are in Saudi Arabia, United Arab Emirates, Sultanate of Oman, and Egypt.


    Our Vision

    Is to bridge the gap between our investors and the African markets with purpose of laying foundation to be part of the incoming Africa’s business revolution.


    Our Mission

    One of the main reasons we decided to participate in the African market is the continent fast growing associated with fast urbanization. All and above, more than 50% of Africans population are under the age of 19 which gives the area the priority to be Interactive over the next 20 to 30 years.

    As combining the Population and the disposable income growth it shows an exponential growth rather than linear growth. Where, nowadays, there are very few places in the world where an investor can actually get those two factors coming strongly together.

    One of our missions is to educate our investors about the potential opportunities that Africa has and to correct any misconceptions by illustrating the difference between perception of risk versus the reality on the ground. It’s essential for coming investments to learn that They would come into a market with less competition. For there’s a big difference between perceived risk and the reality on the ground.

    Why should we cooperate with the local authorities

    We target markets in countries which all have less political risk and volatility. Therefore, we seek to cooperate with the local authorities to guarantee the following:

    • Benefitting from the latest market opportunities.
    • Understanding threats to investors operations and investments and insuring protection for our investors against future risks.
    • Gaining insight into emerging trends that could support, strengthen or disrupt investors activities in the market.

    Getting a full view of the competitive landscape to assess local and international market in order to participate in developing the existing projects by providing new, special skills including technical, marketing, and financial. Focusing on adding new products, patents, property or production capabilities, and increasing the capitals of existing projects. Our investors participation will be reflected in developing new product lines, opening new markets, expanding existing facilities, equipment or inventory, and hiring additional employees.
